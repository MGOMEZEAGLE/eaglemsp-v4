Accessibility Statement

Last updated: June 2026

Eagle Network Solutions is committed to making eaglemsp.com accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. We believe the web should be usable by all, and we work to remove barriers that prevent interaction with or access to our site.

Our Standard

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at the AA level. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with a wide range of disabilities, including visual, auditory, physical, speech, cognitive, and neurological needs.

What We Do

  • Use semantic HTML and clear heading structure for screen readers
  • Provide descriptive alternative text for meaningful images
  • Maintain strong color contrast between text and background
  • Ensure interactive elements are reachable and operable by keyboard
  • Use legible font sizes and generous spacing for readability
  • Design touch targets that are large enough to use comfortably

Ongoing Effort

Accessibility is an ongoing commitment, not a one-time project. As we add pages and features, we test and refine them. Some content from third parties or older material may not yet fully meet our standard, and we are working to address it.

Need Help or Found a Barrier?

If you have trouble accessing any part of this site, or you have a suggestion to improve accessibility, we want to hear from you. Contact us at eaglemsp.com/contact or call 800-704-6165, and we will work with you to provide the information, service, or accommodation you need.

Feedback

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of our website. Your input helps us improve for everyone.
